The Project

A Bold New Identity

Bowdark Consulting came to us ready to grow. As a DFW-based IT consulting firm with a sharp focus on helping businesses turn ideas into reality, they needed a visual identity and brand voice that matched the quality of their work.

We partnered with the Bowdark team to conduct a thorough brand audit and competitive analysis, then built a refreshed identity from the ground up. The new brand system included a refined color palette, typography standards, and motion aesthetic, giving a new level of consistency and professionalism to their entire brand.

What We Delivered

End-to-End Brand System

Brand Identity System

A complete visual identity including primary and secondary logo variations, a refined color palette, and a typography system built to scale across print and digital channels.

Brand Guidelines

A comprehensive brand standards document covering logo usage, color application, typography rules, and real-world usage examples. Now the entire team is on the same page and looking more modern than ever.

Social Content Strategy

A brand launch social media plan including content pillars, post templates, and a launch campaign to introduce the refreshed identity to Bowdark's audience with confidence and consistency.
