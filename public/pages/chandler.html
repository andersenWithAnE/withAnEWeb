The Project

A Website as Bold as the Work Itself

Chandler Studios is a creative powerhouse — a multimedia studio known for its distinctive visual style, high-end collectible art, and a community of passionate fans. When they needed a website that could match the ambition and personality of their work, they turned to With An E Creative.

We designed and developed a bespoke website from the ground up, one that functions as both a portfolio showcase and an e-commerce destination. Every detail — from the typography to the micro-interactions to the overall architecture — was crafted to reflect the bold, nostalgic-yet-modern aesthetic that makes Chandler Studios unmistakable.

The result is a digital home that earns trust, converts visitors, and gives the team a platform they're genuinely proud to share.

What We Delivered

Design, Development, and Beyond

Website Design

A full custom design in Figma — desktop and mobile — built around Chandler's unique visual identity. Every screen was crafted with intentional hierarchy, generous whitespace, and bold typography.

Web Development

Clean, performant front-end development that brings the design to life with smooth animations, responsive layouts, and fast load times — built to scale as the studio grows.

UX Strategy & CMS

Thoughtful information architecture and a content management system setup that empowers the Chandler team to update their portfolio, add products, and publish content without touching a line of code.
